Menus & Tent Cards

Counter pieces that survive the rush.

Menus and tent cards live on the counter, the table and the pickup station — handled, wiped down and refilled daily. We print them on durable cover stocks with laminate or UV coating so they shrug off fingerprints, spills and steam, and reprint cleanly every time the lineup changes.

Production options

Easy to update, easy to keep clean

Lineups change with the season, so we keep menu files set up for fast reprints — swap the items, re-proof, and run. For high-touch pieces we recommend a double-sided laminate or UV coating that wipes clean between services. Print is full CMYK with optional PMS spot for exact brand color across every counter piece.
