Packaging

Boxes built to carry your reputation home.

Packaging is the only part of your brand a customer takes with them. We design and print bakery boxes, cake and pastry cartons, sleeves and inserts on grease-resistant, food-safe board — die-cut to fit your product and finished to survive the trip from case to kitchen table.

Printing

  • Full CMYK process + PMS spot color

  • One-color kraft for a craft look

Finishing

  • Custom die-cutting & steel-rule dies

  • Foil, emboss & spot UV accents
  • Scoring, folding & gluing
Production options

Specified for direct food contact

For anything touching product, we specify food-contact-rated board, inks and coatings and keep print clear of direct-contact surfaces where required. We start from your product dimensions to build a die-line with correct glue tabs, tuck depth and bleed — then supply a folded white dummy or printed proof so you can check fit before tooling and the run.
