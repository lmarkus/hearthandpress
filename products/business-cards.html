Business Cards

Business cards that taste like your brand looks.

Specifications

Stocks, sizes & finishes

Standard sizes

  • 3.5″ × 2″ standard US
  • 3.5″ × 2″ square-corner or rounded
  • 2.5″ × 2.5″ square
  • Slim 3.5″ × 1.75″
  • Custom die-cut shapes

Stocks

  • 14pt & 16pt C2S (coated two sides)
  • 18pt & 32pt premium heavyweight
  • Uncoated natural & kraft
  • Triple-layer with color seam
  • 100% recycled options

Coatings & lamination

  • Soft-touch / suede laminate
  • Matte & gloss laminate
  • Spot UV (high-gloss accents)
  • AQ & satin aqueous

Specialty finishing

  • Foil stamping (gold, copper, holographic)
  • Blind & registered embossing
  • Edge painting & color core
  • Rounded corners (¼″ / ⅛″ radius)
Production options

Built to spec

All cards print full CMYK with optional PMS spot color for exact brand matching. Supply art with a 0.125″ bleed and a 0.125″ safe margin; we preflight every file and return a press-ready proof before we run. Need a hand? Our prepress team sets up bleed, traps and die-lines for you.

Color

CMYK process plus optional PMS spot color and metallic inks.

File setup

PDF/X with 0.125″ bleed, fonts outlined, 300 dpi imagery.

Sides

Single- or double-sided; mix finishes front to back.

Proofing

Digital soft proof standard; hard proof on request.

Bakery use cases

  • Owner & manager cards with foil logo
  • Cake-decorator portfolio cards
  • Front-of-house & catering contacts
  • Loyalty / punch cards on durable stock
  • Gift & appointment cards
  • Market-stall & pop-up contact cards
