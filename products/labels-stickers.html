Labels & Stickers

Labels that brand the jar and clear the rules.

A label does double duty for a bakery: it carries your brand and it carries the legally required ingredient and allergen information. We print die-cut roll labels, jar wraps, closure seals and logo stickers on food-safe facestocks and adhesives — so they look the part and hold up to the fridge, the freezer and the display case.

Compliance & production

Room for the rules, polish for the brand

Ingredient declarations, allergen call-outs, net weight and "made in a facility" statements all need legible space at minimum type sizes. We lay out labels so the regulated panel is clear and compliant while your logo and color still lead. Print is full CMYK plus optional PMS spot; variable data handles batch, lot and best-by codes across a single run.
